Edu4India

Edu4India is first-of-its-kind program designed by International Centre for Technological Innovations (ICTI) to promote digital education and e-learning. Edu4India provides practice exercises, online study materials and personalized dashboard to support students in self evaluation and self learning. Our specially designed modules for teachers help the teachers to provide digitally enabled learning experience and better time management.

"Our Mission is to provide quality digital learning opportunity for all"

During last decade the education system all over the world has gone through a dramatic change towards digitalization. Digital learning tools enable students to develop effective self-directed learning skills and increase their productivity. In addition to that e-learning sharpen critical thinking skills and problem solving skills. 

In India penetration of digital technologies in education sector is mostly limited to cities and upper segments of the society


 Latest analysis by IIT-Guwahati shows that 3 out of 4 students who are getting admissions into premier institutes in India are coming from cities.

Internet penetration, availability and affordability of quality digital materials are the  major challenges faced by rural areas. Edu4India is an attempt made by ICTI  to promote digital education in rural areas and small towns to shape the future of our coming generations for better
