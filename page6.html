Edu4India Scholarship examination is a unique initiative to promote digital education and to help students to test and evaluate their subject knowledge. This test covers areas in science, Mathematics, Logical reasoning and Mental Ability for each class. This test will provide real experience in competitive examinations and help them to build their self-confidence.

EXAMINATION PATTERN

Edu4India scholarship examination is an online multiple choice examination. Examination will be conducted in two levels.

Level One:- Mastery in Science Subjects will be tested

Level Two:- knowledge in Mathematics concepts, Logical Reasoning and Mental Abilities will be tested.

Test duration for each level is 30 minutes


REGISTRATION- STUDENT

Register online using valid mobile number and e-mail id. Login details for Examination will be e-mail to your registered e-mail address one week prior to examination. Only one registration per candidate is allowed, any multiple registration will automatically leads to disqualification.

REGISTRATION- SCHOOL

Schools that are interested in arranging Edu4India Scholarship Examination for their students can register online using school e-mail id/ principal e-mail id. We will be e-mailing login details for all students to the registered school one week prior to the examination date.

                      Last date for registration is 23rd Nov 2017


EXAMINATION SCHEDULES

Each examination has a window of seven days and candidates can take the examination anytime during this window. First level examination is scheduled from 24th November to 1st December and Second level is scheduled from 2nd December to 9th December. 

Please note:- Each candidate will get a unique set of questions and Only one attempt per candidate is allowed

AWARDS AND SCHOLARSHIPS

Results

Results of the examination will be published on 15th December.

Best School Award

Best Digitally Empowered School award will be issued to school with highest no of participation subject to a minimum participation of 200 registrations  or 75%  total strength.

Scholarships

Scholarship amount of Rs 1,000/-  and Merit Certificate will be awarded to top 2 students in each class.
Merit Certificate will be awarded to top 10 students in each class.

Free Online Study Materials

Free online study material for each class will be provided to all the registered students to promote digital learning.

MORE INFO

 

ELIGIBILITY

All the students from Class 6th to Class 12th are eligible for the examination.


All Students from ICSE, CBSE and State syllabus can attend the examination. 

FEES

This examination is FREE for all students.

SYLLABUS

Syllabus for the examination for each class is based on the academic curricula of respective classes.

Our specially designed questions will address CBSE,ICSE and state syllabus students.

                                 

LANGUAGE

Exam will be only in English language. However we are encouraging students from Malayalam medium also to participate the examination
